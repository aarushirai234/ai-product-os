# AI Product OS ⚡

Open-source AI workflows for product teams.

AI Product OS is a collection of practical AI workflows that help product teams automate and improve the messy parts of product work.

The goal is to make it easier to:

- 👥 understand users
- 🕵️ track competitors
- 🧩 synthesize feedback
- 📝 turn research into product specs
- 🚦 review launch risks
- 📊 make sense of messy signals

## Why this exists

Product teams already have more information than they can process.

User feedback lives across Reddit, app reviews, support tickets, interviews, dashboards, customer calls, and social conversations.

The hard part is not collecting more signals.

The hard part is turning messy signals into clear product decisions.

AI Product OS explores how AI can help with that.

## First workflow: Reddit Insight Agent 🔎

The first workflow analyzes Reddit conversations and turns them into PM-ready product insights.

It is designed to identify:

- recurring user complaints
- product themes
- sentiment
- severity
- source-backed evidence
- possible PM actions

## Principles

- 🛠️ Build practical workflows, not toy demos
- 🔗 Ground outputs in evidence
- 🔍 Make results easy to inspect
- 🧠 Keep humans in the decision loop
- 🎯 Optimize for real product decisions
